Details
How to find your meter serial number
Your meter serial number (MSN) is usually printed on the front of your meter. It's a mix of letters and numbers, typically 10 characters long. You can also find it on a recent energy bill or in your online account.

Understanding your bill

A breakdown of the charges on your energy bill

Your energy bill is made up of several components including the unit rate, standing charge, VAT at 5%, and any applicable discounts from your tariff.

Switching tariff

How to change your current energy tariff

You can switch tariff at any time through your online account. If you're currently on a fixed tariff, early exit fees may apply.
